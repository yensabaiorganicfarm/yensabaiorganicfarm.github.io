Yensabai Cafe

We boil water for our tea and coffee on a wood fire, so there is time for you to relax and cool down after hiking around. Enjoy the peaceful atmosphere of Yensabai Organic Farm.
We share 5% of the profit from the farmstay and cafe with the local community.
