Nearby Attractions

Visitors can go hiking to the Tad Mok waterfall & view point in the nearby hills, tourists need to pay a small fee in the village. Scenic boat trips on the Nam Ou river are also available. 
We are planning to make a tourist package for our customers as well. It will include a homestay, food, cooking class, bamboo handicrafts class, hiking, gardening or farm experience, and a community tour.
