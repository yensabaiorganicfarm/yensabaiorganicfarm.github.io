Cooking Classes

I love to cook and teach Lao cuisine to tourists.
If you would like to learn traditional Lao cuisine you are welcome at my farm. You can book the course by sending me an e-mail.
If you are going to visit Moung Ngoi Neua first, you can book the course in the village at "Geko Bar" from my cousin Kai.
