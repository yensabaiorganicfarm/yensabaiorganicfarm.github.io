Farmstay and Camping

You are welcome to stay as a guest at Yensabai Organic Farm. We have camping tents and bungalows for tourists. Staying here is a unique experience of farm lifestyle and Lao culture. You will learn many things at the farm, such as gardening, cooking, animal feeding, building, etc.
The surrounding area provides a great variety of activities, for example a refreshing bath in the Tad Mok Waterfalls (around 25 minutes walking), fishing experience at the river or a hike to neighboring mountains and viewpoints.

Bangalows: 29 usd/person per night
Tents (in an open house or outside): 19 usd/person per night
These prices include 3 meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner) and learning activities at the farm.
Booking is available via Paypal or internet banking QR code directly.

For any further information concerning accommodation, availabilities and booking, please contact us via email to: yensabaifarm@gmail.com. 

We share 5% of the profit from the farmstay & cafe with the local community.
