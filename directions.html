How to get here

Yensabai Organic  Farm is located 15 minute walk from Sop Kong, a small village halfway between Nong Kiaw and Moung Ngoi towns. You can take a boat from either town (around 50,000 Lao Kip/person) and tell the driver to drop you off at Sop Kong village. Then ask villagers my name (Xay) and they will point you towards my farm. Once you come off the boat go straight through the village, along the path until you see a sign that says "turn right" and then follow the path until you get here.
(If arriving from Dien Bien Phu, Vietnam, you can take a boat downriver Nam Ou from Muang Khua town.)
My farm is also on Google Maps or either on Maps.Me

I can arrange a private transfer by car or boat (car is not available during the rainy season). Price: 20 usd/car/boat
